BDSM Dating – ALT.com

ALT is a niche dating site designed for people interested in fetishes and alternative fantasies that go beyond the scope of traditional dating sites.

    ALT is a fetish-based dating site that offers dating, match-up and hookup services for people interested in connecting with others who share similar fetish and alternative fantasy interests. In addition to looking for hookups and possible long-term relationships, users can also simply use the site to communicate with others in the alternative community using the site’s message board and article systems.

  • Key Features

    ALT has notable key features which help users connect with others on the site who are interested in exploring the same fetishes and fantasies. The more important features include:

    • Messaging system with customizable options, including auto reply, block function, and filter system to reject users who do not meet your standards

    • Hotlist creation to connect with specific members who will then be able to contact you
    • Cupid Report feature will let you see existing and new members you've been matched with
    • Friend Network community center where groups can talk about a variety of topics
    • Private chatrooms for exclusive chats with select people
    • Blog hosting on site allows users to create their own blogs

  • Pros and Cons

    Like any site, ALT has some pros and cons that you should consider before making your decision.

    Pros

    • • Large active membership base ensures you'll make matches and connections
    • • Community interaction is high and lets you get connected with the alternative community
    • • Free membership options available
    • • Fetish and alternative fantasy niche means you’ll be able to meet likeminded people

    Cons

    • • Free accounts are highly limited
    • • Website interface is visually dark and not aesthetically pleasing to see for long periods of time on end
    • • Notably an abundance of fake free account profiles to wade through

  • Conclusion

    ALT is a highly niched dating site that is best for users who are specifically interested in fetish and alternative fantasy hookups, relationships and other forms of communications. Regular users will not benefit much from this site due to the fetish-oriented material. The site does offer free membership but users are better off springing for paid membership due to the limited options for free members in regards to messaging, using the community chatrooms, and more. Despite an outdated web site, ALT was—and continues to be—the only major source of online dating for people in the alternative community.
