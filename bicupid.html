BiCupid is a bisexual based dating site that is ideal for bisexual adults who are interested in forming a variety of adult connections, ranging from casual relationships to dating to long-term relationships and everything in between.

  • About

    BiCupid is a fairly traditional dating site that caters specifically to bisexual users. The site’s membership is comprised of bisexual adults who are interested in finding matches of either gender for hookups, casual encounters, dating, and even potentially forming long-term relationships meant to last. BiCupid operates like a standard dating site, with the ability to create profiles, search for other members, and contact other members whom you think may be a good match.

  • Key Features

    BiCupid is a fairly traditional and almost old-school style dating site. It allows for both individual users and couples to sign up for the service; users can create profiles, upload photos, and even generate "first date ideas" which other members can browse and possibly use as a springboard for connecting with other users. BiCupid also features community forums so users can post a variety of topics and comments, as well as a blog with information about dating, the bisexual experience, and much more.
    The site also has a unique "Let's Meet" feature which allows for users to approve or deny users based on their profile picture alone. This is a somewhat Tinder-style feature that offers a minimalistic alternative to the traditional way to use the site.
    Free members can only browse the membership base and send "Winks." A paid membership is required for all other features on the site, including sending and reading messages, participating on the forums, using the Let’s Meet feature and more.

  • Pros and Cons

    There are pros and cons to BiCupid; the following are the most significant Pros and Cons that users should consider before signing up for the site.

    Pros

    • • Site is designed especially for bisexual users, making it easy to find matches with similar preferences
    • • Individual and couple memberships make it easier to connect with people interested in threesomes
    • • First date idea browsing is unique and fun
    • • Let's Meet feature lets you use the site in a more casual way

    Cons

    • • Website is old fashioned and a bit clunky compared to modern dating sites
    • • No algorithm that matches users based on personality
    • • Free membership is very limited

  • Conclusion

    BiCupid is a solid, if old fashioned, dating site which will appeal to people looking to make bisexual (and/or bisexual threesome) connections with other adults. While free members will likely want to upgrade, the paid membership has enough features to make the cost worthwhile. The site has features that will appeal both to people looking for long term relationships as well as people hoping for more casual, Tinder-style connections.
